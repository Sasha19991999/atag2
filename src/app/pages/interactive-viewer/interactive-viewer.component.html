Protect the environment. Green Mode

To achieve the best washing and rinsing results, while reducing water and energy consumption, each stage of the washing program is extended. The total program time is therefore longer compared to normal mode.

Child lock

Keep your children safe: lock the control panel so that the washing machine does not start accidentally.

Sensi SaveTM

The Sensi Save™ feature reduces the carbon footprint by calculating how much water and energy each load needs. The optimized washing program also saves your valuable time. Meaningful, right?

Allergy mode

In this mode, extra water is added to rinse all detergent residues from the laundry and the detergent compartment. More water is used in Allergy mode and energy and time are also added to achieve an optimal wash and rinse result. The selected temperature is kept at the same level for a longer period of time.

Door Seal

ATAG washing machines do not have a rubber door sleeve/door seal. And there's a good reason for that. The door seal can break down over time and dirt can accumulate in it. By eliminating these, you can now wash more hygienically. Our washing machines are equipped with the SteelSeal™. Dirt and bacteria don't stand a chance.

Steam

Steam is fresh and convenient. Steam helps freshen up your laundry in no time, without the use of detergent. After 20 minutes of steam treatment with our Steam Refresh program, there is only a short drying time left before you can use your reborn garment. Steam is a very effective and gentle way to treat your shirts, jackets and trousers. You can steam both natural fibres, such as cotton and linen, and synthetic fabrics. The shirt program has an integrated steam function. This function adds steam at the end of the wash cycle to smooth out creases and reduce creases that may appear on fabrics after spinning.

Eco Connection

Having access to sustainable and economical hot springs can save energy by connecting your machine directly to the water source. Ideal for district heating, heat pumps and solar panels.

Full color display

The TFT color display guides you in making the right choices with every wash. The display, as well as the rest of the machine, has low energy consumption.

Dose Assist

Dose Assist function makes you stop worrying about using the right amount of detergent for your wash. Instead of struggling with dosing the right amount, your ASKO washing machine recommends the required amount of detergent. With the Dose Assist function of our machines, the correct amount of detergent is automatically added based on the load, degree of soiling, water hardness, program type and temperature. This way you always use the right amount, so no more overdosing.

Quattro Construction™

The Quattro Construction™ is a unique but simple system consisting of four shock absorbers that transfer the vibration energy from the outer drum to the bottom plate of the machine. In fact, the entire construction is inside the housing and even at maximum spin there are virtually no vibrations. Ideal for a wooden floor.

Pro Wash™

The Pro Wash™ system moves the detergent mixed with water towards the top of the drum and lets it land on the laundry from the top. Thus, the laundry is efficiently soaked to guarantee a perfect result, even with a short washing cycle.
